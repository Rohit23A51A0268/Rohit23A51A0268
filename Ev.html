About EV Friend

Introduction

Welcome to EV Friend, your reliable partner in finding and booking EV recharge
slots with ease. Our platform connects EV holders with recharge bunk holders,
making the recharging process seamless and efficient.

Our Mission

At EV Friend, our mission is to simplify the recharging experience for electric vehicle
owners and support the growth of sustainable transportation. We aim to create a
network of trusted recharge points that are easily accessible and convenient for everyone.

Our Story

EV Friend was born out of a desire to address the challenges faced by EV owners in
finding available recharge slots. Inspired by the increasing demand for electric
vehicles and the need for a more organized recharging system, we set out to
develop a solution that benefits both EV holders and recharge bunk operators.

Our Future Plans

We are committed to continuously improving EV Friend by adding more features
and expanding our network of recharge points. Stay tuned for exciting updates!
